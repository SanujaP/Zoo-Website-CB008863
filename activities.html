Activities

Dehiwala National Zoological Gardens has its on location activities which would show up the well trained Animal acts. These acts consists few animals in the zoo, and they have been given special attention and trained well for their specific acts. No animal was harmed during trainings or while performing except for the ways of guiding. We ensure that we provide a high class animal for our visitors to enjoy and we warmly welcome all of our visitors to get access for these special shows by purchasing show tickets on location. We hope that all our visitors would be well entertained by the animal acts.
