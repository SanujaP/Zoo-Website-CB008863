Reptile Kingdom

Sri Lanka is well diversed with the reptile species. Reptiles consists of lizards, crocodiles, tortises, turtles and snakes. More than 100 species of reptiles are endemic to Sri Lanka and approximately 32 species of reptiles inclusive of foreign species could be seen in the Reptile Kingdom. A wide range of Reptiles could be seen at the zoo specially, crocodiles, pythons, tortises, turtles, different types of snakes and rhinos are exhibted at zoological gardens.
