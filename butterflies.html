Butterfly Garden

Almost 245 species of butterflies have been recorded in Sri Lanka, of which 26 species are endemic and 100 species are threatened. Thye zoological garden has separated a space for the butterflies to have their freedom and do what they love. This has been one of the most attracted parts of the zoo since it was formed. Around 30 butterfly species could be seen by the visitors once they enter the Butterfly Garden. We warmly welcome all of our visitors to ccome and witness this moment which will be a promised delicate for the day at the zoo.
