Types of Animals

The National Zoological Gardens Dehiwala currently has 72 species of Mammals, 65 species of Birds, 31 species of Reptiles, 89 species of Fish, 3 Families of Amphibians and 30 speecies of Butterflies and many more animals in the zoo. The zoo accomadates around 2500-3500 animals and it may be changed due to breeding and free living colonies of birds. Visitors has the freedom to look at any animal they want to, but have strict restrictions against feeding animals and harming animals.

Types of Plants

Sri Lanka has a great climatic difference in each and every area. Due to this, Sri Lanka has a great diversityv among plants. Since there are mountains, low lands, hills and even rain-forests Sri Lanka's beauty too has been increased due to the diversity of plants. There are over 7,500 plants in the country and out of them more than 800 species are eendemic species. The special endemic plants could be seen in the low country and mountainous areas in the country. In Sri Lanka different types of plants such as Medicinal plants, Plants for natural dyes, Wlid relatives of cultivated plants and Forest type plants. These plants has helped the country to increase its value by supplying food resources, medicinal value, commercial value, ecological value, aesthetic value, supply fire wood and etc.
Dehiwala zoological Gardens has made it a point to protect and help them to diversify by having them in the zoological gardens. We warmly welcome all our visitors to come and have a look at these beautiful plants which could only be seen in Sri Lanka.
