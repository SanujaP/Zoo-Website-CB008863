The Sliders Show

Dehiwala National Zoological Gardens presents The Sliders Show, an entertaining show presented by snakes of the zoological gardens. Our visitors will be able to have a special time with these sliding friends, and visitors will have the oppurtunity to touch them and make a life time memory with the snakes. This is a special show since this will be shown for free of charge.\
Please note that this show is free of charge.



Fee Per Person - LKR 0.00 (Free of charge)
Start Time - 2.30 PM
Show Days - Saturday and Sunday
