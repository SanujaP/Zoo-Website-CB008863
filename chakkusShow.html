Chakku's Show

Dehiwala National Zoological Gardens presents Chakku's Show, an entertaining show presented by well trained playful Chakku the Sea lion. Chakku would be doing extreme acts which would amaze all the visitors. Furthermore these animals will bring joy to our visitors so that we will be able to fulfill one of the main goals of our organization as well.
Please note that there will be extra charges for this show, kindly refer to the on location ticket counter for purchasings.


Fee Per Person - LKR 3000.00
Start Time - 4.00 PM
Show Days - Every Working Day
