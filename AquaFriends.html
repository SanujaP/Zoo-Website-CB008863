Aqua Friends

The Aquarium of the National Zoological gardens has been one of the key factors of the zoo. The aquarium was established 40 years ago and the ccollection has been increasing since then. In the aquarium visitors would be able to experience many fish species and corals. There are over 50 species of fish in the aquarium and many types of corals in 22 different tanks. All the visitors would be able to experience this beauty once they visit us on location.
