The Cat Family

Sri lanka has it's own species of cats. At the Dehiwala zoo, our visitors has the chance of seeing rare species of the cat family. Sri lankan leopard, African Lion and Bengal tigers could be seen at the zoological gardens. Our visitors could be able to see these carnivores who plays a crucial role in the zoo and in the jungle among other animals. Visit us on location to experience the roars and the behaviour of the Cat Family.
