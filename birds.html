Birds In The Sky

Sri Lanka is a country which has a wide range of birds around, There are more than 450 species in Sri Lanka. These birds travels constatntly due the change of the climate. Out of 450 plus numbers, 220 plus species are residents including 33 species of endemics who makes an avian diversity of in the island. Dehiwala Zoological Gardens has a wide range of birds including flamingos, ostriches, cassowary and Emus who are around the park. We also have a collection of semi aquatic birds such as swans, night herons, cormorant, egrets and spotted billed pelicans and etc. To add more color to the zoological garden, macaws, pheasants and love birds too could be seen in the park while rare birds such as eagles and owls too could be seen at the same sight. We warmly welcome all our visitors to come and witness these eye-catching animals.
